Welcome to the help! It describes the various functionality of the app and how the Lamp "should" react to it.

Enable SLamp Switch

  • Turns on the Lamp

  • The colour will be what the mixer currently shows

  • Will automatically toggle "ON" when some other functions are utilised

Daylight Alarm Switch

  • Will notify the Lamp to automatically turn on 30min before your next phone alarm

  • Reads the value of the next upcoming alarm. Make sure your next alarm is correct! Just toggle the switch again or use the Sync Alarms Button to update the alarm time

  • The colour will be what the mixer currently shows. Be prepared to wake up to blood red if you don't change it!

Sync Alarms Button

  • Reads the value of the next upcoming alarm and sends it to the Lamp

  • Useful if you change the alarm on your phone and the Daylight Alarm Switch is already set

  • It is mostly there to look cool...

Daylight Color Switch

  • Sets the colour to some RGB value vaguely representing sunlight

  • Leave this "ON" after switching the Daylight Alarm to wake up the warm glow of artificial sunlight. mmm warm...

  • Will automatically toggle off when you change the colour

Party Mode Switch

  • Who knows what this one will do??

  • Well it should put the Lamp into a mode where it randomly sets the colour at short random intervals of time and maybe even randomly turns on and off smooth transitions. No guarantees on anything with this one.
